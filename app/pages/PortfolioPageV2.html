Case Study

Reliable Automation in a Real-World SaaS

Splitify requires perfect timing. Users need recurring bills, automatic reminders, and timezone-accurate due dates — all without batching errors or repeated sends.

1 The Problem

Complex date arithmetic caused drift. Users needed local-time accuracy for due dates to prevent "phantom" late fees or confusion, preventing repeated reminder spam.

2 My Approach

  • • Built "pseudo-PST" logic for timezone normalization.
  • • Created idempotent scheduler to prevent duplicates.
  • • Custom libs: calculateDueDate, sameOrAfterDay.

3 The Impact

Zero duplicate reminders sent in production. Billing cycles remain accurate regardless of month length. Participants pay faster due to frictionless flow.
